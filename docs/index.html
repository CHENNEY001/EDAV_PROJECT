Chapter 1 Introduction

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease and people infected with it will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness, death is rare. There is no expedient medication or treatment for recovering. Besides keeping social distances, wearing masks, etc., one of the most effective way for people not contaminating by COVID-19 is to take COVID-19 vaccine. People are encouraged to get COVID-19 vaccine by government and world health organization during the pandemic. We are now predicted to be at the end of COVID-19 pandemic due to high vaccination rate.
